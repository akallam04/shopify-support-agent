# Shipping Policy

Aurora Outfitters ships from our warehouse in Denver, Colorado. Everything below applies to orders placed on our online store.

## Processing times

Orders placed before 12:00 pm Mountain Time on a business day ship the same or next business day. Orders placed after the cutoff, or on weekends and holidays, begin processing the next business day. During holiday peaks (late November through December) allow one extra business day.

## Domestic shipping options

Standard shipping is free on orders over 75 USD and 6.95 USD below that, arriving in 3 to 6 business days. Expedited shipping is 14.95 USD and arrives in 2 business days. Overnight shipping is 29.95 USD and arrives the next business day when the order is placed before the 12:00 pm MT cutoff.

## International shipping

We currently ship internationally to Canada only. Canadian orders ship for a flat 19.95 USD and arrive in 7 to 12 business days. Import duties and taxes are calculated at the border and are the recipient's responsibility. We do not yet ship to other countries.

## Tracking your order

You will receive an email with a tracking number as soon as your order ships. Tracking can take up to 24 hours to show movement after the label is created. You can also ask our support team for your order status at any time.

## Address changes

We can change a shipping address within 2 hours of the order being placed, as long as the order has not entered processing. Contact support immediately with your order number and the corrected address.

## Delayed or lost packages

If tracking has not updated for 7 days on a domestic shipment, or 21 days on a Canadian shipment, contact us and we will open a carrier investigation. If the carrier confirms the package is lost, we will send a replacement or issue a full refund, your choice.
