# Returns and Exchanges

We want you happy with your gear. If something is not right, here is exactly how returns work at Aurora Outfitters.

## Return window

You have 30 days from the delivery date to start a return. Items must be unworn and unused, with original tags attached and original packaging included. Items showing wear, wash, or outdoor use cannot be accepted.

## How to start a return

Email support@auroraoutfitters.com with your order number and the items you want to return. For US orders we reply within 1 business day with a prepaid return label and instructions. Canadian customers ship returns at their own cost to our Denver warehouse.

## Refunds

Refunds go to the original payment method within 5 to 7 business days after we receive and inspect the return. Original shipping fees are not refunded unless the return is due to our error. You will receive a confirmation email when the refund is issued.

## Return shipping cost

A flat 7.50 USD return shipping fee is deducted from your refund when you use our prepaid label. The fee is waived entirely if the item arrived damaged, defective, or not as ordered.

## Exchanges

Size or color exchanges are free the first time: reply to your return confirmation with the size or color you want and we ship it once the original is scanned by the carrier. For the fastest turnaround, you can instead return for a refund and place a new order right away.

## Final sale items

Gift cards and items marked FINAL SALE at checkout cannot be returned or exchanged. Ski wax and other consumables that have been opened are also non-returnable.

## Damaged or defective items

If your order arrives damaged or defective, email photos of the item and packaging within 7 days of delivery. We will replace it or refund you in full, including all shipping fees, and you keep or recycle the damaged item as we advise.

## Warranty

Aurora Outfitters brand gear carries a 1-year warranty against manufacturing defects from the date of delivery. Normal wear and tear, misuse, and accident damage are not covered. Warranty claims follow the same photo process as damaged items.
