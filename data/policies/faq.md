# Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to the questions our support team hears most.

## Price adjustments

If an item you bought drops in price within 14 days of your order date, we will refund the difference to your original payment method, once per order. Adjustments do not apply between different sale events or to FINAL SALE items.

## Gift cards

Gift cards are digital and delivered by email, usually within minutes of purchase. They are available in 10, 25, 50, and 100 USD denominations. They never expire, carry no fees, and can be used across multiple orders. Gift cards cannot be refunded or exchanged for cash. To check a balance, contact support with the gift card code.

## Order changes and cancellations

We can modify or cancel an order within 2 hours of it being placed, provided it has not entered processing. After that window the order ships as placed, and you can use the normal return process instead.

## Do you have physical stores

We are online only. All orders ship from our warehouse in Denver, Colorado, and we do not offer local pickup at this time.

## Choosing a snowboard size

As a starting point, a board standing between your chin and nose works for most riders. Riders heavier than average for their height should size up a few centimeters, lighter riders size down. Every board's product page lists the exact lengths available, and our support team is happy to recommend a size if you share your height and weight.

## Price matching

We do not match prices from other retailers. We do honor our own price adjustment policy described above.

## Contacting support

Email support@auroraoutfitters.com, Monday through Friday, 8 am to 5 pm Mountain Time. We answer every message within 1 business day. Include your order number for the fastest help.
